# Free Chinese Martial Arts SFX Sample Pack

20 free martial arts sound effects for game prototypes, animation tests, and short video edits.

This is a small original sample pack made to test a focused Chinese martial arts Foley library. The sounds are procedurally synthesized and edited for this pack; they are not copied from commercial sound libraries, films, marketplace bundles, or training videos.

## Included

- Punches
- Kicks
- Cloth and robe movement
- Staff whooshes
- Body impacts
- Synthetic training shouts
